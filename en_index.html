Scientific, educational and applied journal "Financial Research", specialized in methodology, theory and practices of finance, money circulation, credit, securities market, management corporate finance
Financial Research is a peer–reviewed scientific periodical printed publication, it has been published since 2000. Magazine It is the only one in the South of Russia specialized in the field of finance and credit. Publication on the principles of self-financing is carried out by the Rostov State Economic university (RINH), the largest scientific and educational center in the field of finance and credit in the Southern Federal District.
The concept of the journal is focused on theoretical generalization and applied analysis development world and national financial and monetary systems, securities market, as well as identification national and regional specifics, features and consequences of the inclusion of the national and regional financial market into the system of the world financial the market.
The subject of the journal includes theoretical developments, applied research, including including joint with foreign scientists, methodological developments in key financial areas.
The content of the columns of the journal corresponds to the branches of science and groups specialties, by which academic degrees are awarded:

DEAR SCIENTISTS AND GRADUATE STUDENTS!
We invite you to publish an article in a Scientific, educational and applied journal "Financial Research"
The magazine is the only one in the South of Russia specialized in the field of finance and credit. The publication is based on the principles of self-financing carried out by Rostov State University of Economics (RINH), the largest research and training center in the field of finance and credit in The Southern Federal District.

The journal is included in the List of peer-reviewed scientific publications, in which the main scientific results of dissertations should be published for the degree of candidate of sciences, for the degree of Doctors of Sciences, edition 2022

The journal is included in the Russian Science Citation Index (RSCI)

The subject of the journal includes theoretical developments, applied research, in including joint research with foreign scientists, methodological developments on key financial directions.

REQUIREMENTS FOR THE DESIGN OF ARTICLES FOR PUBLICATION IN THE JOURNAL "FINANCIAL RESEARCH"

  1. The material proposed for publication must be original, not previously published in other printed publications, and correspond to the subject The journal is written in the context of modern economic scientific literature, and also contain an obvious element of creating new knowledge. The originality of the text should be for graduate students and applicants – at least 85%. You can check the text on the website http://antiplagiat.ru

  2. The author(s) send to the editorial office 1 (one) copy of the article in printed form, must be signed by the author(s) and in electronic form (by e-mail to: research@inbox.ru). Information in printed and electronic form must contain the following files: information about the author(s), an article with an abstract and keywords, an accompanying letter and consent for review and publication articles.
    For postgraduates (applicants), a review of the scientific manager, with the certified signature of the head

    3. Information about the author(s) contains:

    • title of the article (in Russian and English);
    • Full name of the authors (in full),
    • each author's place of work, position,
    • academic degree, academic title,
    • email address,
    • place of residence (city, country)
    • ORCID

      • More detailed information about the author(s) must be specified in the Questionnaire – (Appendix 1)

  4. Title of the article, full name of the author(s), abstract of the article (from 200 to 250 words, includes: relevance of the topic researches, problem statement, research objectives, research methods, results and key conclusions), keywords (from 8 to 10 words and phrases) , bibliographic lists should be presented in Russian and in English. The list of sources used should not contain less than 12 sources, the average number of sources is 12-15, of which at least 30% there must be foreign sources from Scopus and Web of Science, no more than 20% - self-citation.

    Annotations should not contain references to sections, formulas, figures, numbers of the cited literature.

    The abstract should include the following aspects:

    • relevance of the research topic;
    • problem statement, research objectives;
    • research methods;
    • the results of the work and the scope of the results;
    • key conclusions.

    (according to Application 2)

    *Authors should pay special attention to writing an abstract, both in Russian and in English. The annotation should not repeat the text of the articles. The size of the annotation is from 200 to 250 words.

  5. The volume of the article should not exceed 1 p. l. (40,000 p. characters – 20 pages) A4 typewritten text, including tables, references and drawings (diagrams). Simultaneously with the article, you need to print, fill out, sign and send us the original and scanned Consent to publish the article in the journal. The author's full name should be indicated above the title of the article on the right. (Application 3)
    The authors are also responsible for the accuracy of the submitted publications of information, facts, figures and other information.

  6. Files with the text of the article and information about the author are made in MS WORD format, font – Times New Roman, size – 14 pt., interval – 1. Margins: upper, lower – 2.0 cm, right – 1.5 cm, left – 2 cm.

  7. It is recommended to use the MS Formula Editor to write formulas Equation (version not lower than 3.0.).

  8. Tables, figures and diagrams should be: in black and white, numbered and titled, with links to sources.

  9. For the design of tables and figures, you must use the following parameters: font – Arial, size – 12 pt., interval – 1.

  10. The designations of units of measurement of various quantities (m, kg, rubles, etc.), abbreviations such as "G." (year) should be preceded by an unbroken space sign (see "Insert – "Symbols"), which marks the imposition of a ban on their separation during layout from the number they define. The same applies to the set of initials and surnames.

  11. When using quotation marks in the text, typographic quotation marks ("") are used. A dash is indicated by the symbol "–" (long dash); a hyphen "-".

  12. The bibliographic list is given in the order of mention at the end of the article. The text should contain references in square brackets only to published materials. References to foreign sources are given in a foreign language and are accompanied in the case of translation into Russian with an indication of the translation.

  13. The names of foreign companies and financial institutions are given in the text latin letters without quotes and highlights. After being mentioned in the text surnames of foreign scientists, company executives, etc. in Russian, the spelling of the name and surname in Latin letters is given in parentheses, if this is not followed by a link to the work of a foreign author.

  14. The article must contain the JEL Classifier (JEL Classification Codes) (after the title of the article)

  15. The text of the article should be structured according to the format IMRaD - Introduction (Introduction), Materials and methods (Materials and methods), Results (Results), Discussion (Discussion), Conclusion (Conclusion).

IMPORTANT! Each Application is issued in a separate file with the names "Questionnaire of the Twins", "Article of the Twins", "the cover letter of the Kuznetsovs", "The review of the Kuznetsovs". Cover letter (Appendix 3) is signed and sent in scanned form.

A package of documents issued not according to the requirements for registration and consideration not accepted.

To correctly indicate the information in English, please use transliteration systems. When making bibliographic references in English, it is necessary to specify official English-language names of journals, that is, fixed either in the Charter magazine, or at the ISSN International Center, or in the Russian Science Citation Index.

For example: Financial Research – Financial Research (registered name at the ISSN International Center). Design of bibliographic references and annotations in accordance with the above this will significantly increase the citation of authors, whose articles are published in the journal "Financial Research".
The Editorial Board reserves the right to shorten if necessary articles, subjecting them to editorial editing.

Editor-in-Chief's page
The editor-in-chief of the journal "Financial Research" Kuznetsov Nikolay Gennadievich, Doctor of Economics, Professor, Honored Scientist of the Russian Federation
Dear authors and colleagues!
Let me thank the authors, reviewers, and the editorial board of the journal for their scientific competence and the high quality of the materials provided! The editorial board of the journal "Financial Research" also thanks the authors and readers for fruitful cooperation in the preparation of original, high-quality scientific articles that analyze theoretical and practice-oriented strategic objectives of the development of the country's economy. The editorial board of the journal will give preference to scientific articles containing non-standard, innovative ideas that will serve as the basis for professional discussion on the pages of the journal. But our plans are feasible only in full understanding of the authors with the requirements of the editorial board of the journal for the preparation of articles with relevant topics corresponding to the May Decrees of the President of the Russian Federation and national projects. The priority topic of the journal also includes the institutional environment of the Russian economy, adapting to the new strategic vector that defines the digital economy. The journal's publications are aimed at scientists, analysts, the business community, government officials, graduate students, students and readers who show professional interest in the patterns of socio-economic development of our country and the world economy.

RULES FOR REVIEWING SCIENTIFIC ARTICLES
Each article submitted to the editorial board of the journal "Financial Research" must undergo a review procedure. This procedure is anonymous for both the author and the reviewer. As reviewers, the editorial board of the journal attracts members of the Editorial Board, as well as external experts. The reviewer must have an academic degree of candidate or Doctor of sciences or practical experience in this field. All reviewers are recognized experts on the subject of peer-reviewed materials and have in during the last 3 years of publication on the subject of the reviewed article.

  • After receiving the article by the editorial board, it is registered, reviewed by the Editorial Board and sent for examination specialists in the appropriate profile.
  • The reviewer should evaluate:

    • scientific level of the material;
    • relevance of the issues covered;
    • novelty and informativeness;
    • matching the volume to the selected topic.

  • The expert's conclusion in the review should meet the following requirements:

    • should the material be published;
    • should I return it to the author(s) for revision;
    • whether to send the material for additional review (indicating possible reviewers or their profile);
    • or reject the material as unsuitable for publications (with reasons for refusal).

  • When recommending "publish the material after revision ...", comments (requirements for this material) that are mandatory from the point of view of the reviewer, and comments that can be considered as wishes.

It should be noted, that the set of listed requirements and criteria is of a typical, general nature. Each specific article it is considered taking into account the application of an individual approach.

  • The article can be submitted to the author for revision by e-mail if the review indicates the need for adjustments. In this case, the date of receipt of the manuscript to the Editorial Office is considered date of return of the revised scientific article by the author.

  • A scientific article submitted to the author for corrections should be sent back to the Editorial Office of the journal in a corrected form within a month . The revised manuscript should be accompanied by an explanatory letter from the authors containing answers to all comments and adjustments that have been made.

  • In case of disagreement with the opinion of the reviewer, the author of the article has the right to provide a reasoned response to the Editorial Board of the journal. The article can be sent for re-reviewing or for approval to the Editorial Board or the Editor-in-Chief of the journal "Financial Research". In controversial cases the decision is made by the Editor-in-Chief.

  • The article with changes based on the reviewer's comments is sent to the reviewer for approval. If the article revised by the author and approved by the reviewer satisfies according to the requirements of the Editorial Board, it is published in the journal.

  • The editorial board of the journal "Financial Research" reserves the right to reject articles primarily due to non-compliance with the requirements of the originality of the author's text or in case of inability or unwillingness of the author to take into account the opinion and recommendations of the Editorial Board of the journal. In case of refusal to send a manuscript submitted by the author for review, the Editorial Board of the journal must send the author a reasoned response.

  • As part of the review, the Executive Secretary of the journal "Financial Research" informs the author of the decision, a reasoned response, while the maximum period of the review process of scientific articles between the dates of receipt of the manuscript to the Editorial Office and the decision of the Editorial Board is 2 months. It should be noted that the terms of stay of the article in the Editorial Office of the journal in each individual case are determined taking into account the conditions and the requirements for publishing an article in the next issue of the journal.

  • The presence of a positive review is not formally a sufficient reason for the publication of an article in the journal "Financial Research", since the final decision on the possibility of publishing highly intellectual scientific research materials are accepted by the Editor-in-Chief.
